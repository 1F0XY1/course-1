Effective study:

  1. Set Your Goal: Know what you need to study before you start.
  2. Make a Schedule: Plan when and what you’ll study. Stick to it.
  3. Cut Distractions: Find a quiet spot and put your phone away.
  4. Engage with the Material: Take notes, make flashcards, or explain it to someone.
  5. Review Often: Go over what you’ve learned regularly to keep it fresh.
